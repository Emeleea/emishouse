About

Emi's House is a public list aiming to point to all the resources for trans/queer people one may need in the Greater Toronto Area. It was created by Emilia H, who is a community worker major at George Brown College. She uses she/her pronouns and specializes in Queer/Trans resource collection and supports.

Contact

If you have any suggestions or feedback for this website, please reach Emilia at emilia.harary@gmail.com
