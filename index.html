About

Helloooo! My name is Emi! (she/her/hers)

Emi's House is a website and community established by myself. It is meant to be a side-project amidst my Community Worker journey. This website aims to be an archive of active resources within the Golden Horseshoe area (Windsor to Ottawa), a journal on what I've learned amidst my trans experience, and generally something helpful to point to for those newly transitioning.

Layout heavily inspired by the Melee Library website.
